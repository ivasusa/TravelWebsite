Switzerland - Roof of Europe

Luxury ski vacation in the heart of the Alps

About the Package

Experience the pinnacle of luxury in the Swiss Alps! From the iconic Matterhorn in Zermatt to the glamorous slopes of St. Moritz, this journey combines world-class skiing with Swiss hospitality. Enjoy pristine slopes, luxury chalets, and breathtaking Alpine panoramas in the heart of Europe's most prestigious winter destinations.

Travel Itinerary

Day 1 - Arrival in Zermatt

Flight to Zurich and transfer to Zermatt. Hotel check-in, ski resort orientation and first evening in the Alps.

Day 2-3 - Skiing in Zermatt

Two full days of skiing on the slopes of the Matterhorn. Europe's longest ski runs and spectacular panoramic views.

Day 4 - Zermatt - St. Moritz

Morning skiing in Zermatt. Afternoon scenic Glacier Express train ride to St. Moritz - one of the world's most beautiful train journeys.

Day 5-6 - St. Moritz luxury

Two days of skiing in glamorous St. Moritz. Exclusive spa treatments, fine dining and luxury shopping in this world-renowned resort.

Day 7 - Departure

Free morning for last-minute shopping or spa treatments. Transfer to Zurich airport and flight back to Belgrade.
