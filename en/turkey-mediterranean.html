Turkey - Mediterranean Magic

Historic cities, beautiful beaches and rich culture of Anatolia

About the Package

Experience the magical blend of European and Asian cultures in Turkey. From the bustling streets of Istanbul to the pristine beaches of Antalya and the fairy chimneys of Cappadocia, this journey offers unforgettable memories.

Itinerary

Day 1-3 - Istanbul

Arrival in Istanbul, Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, Topkapi Palace, Grand Bazaar, Bosphorus cruise.

Day 4-5 - Cappadocia

Flight to Cappadocia, hot air balloon ride, underground cities, Goreme Open Air Museum.

Day 6-7 - Antalya

Flight to Antalya, Mediterranean beaches, Antalya old town, Side ancient ruins.

Day 8 - Departure

Free morning, souvenir shopping, departure from Antalya airport.
