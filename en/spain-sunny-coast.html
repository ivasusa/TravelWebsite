Spain - Sunny Coast

Barcelona, Madrid & Costa del Sol – the perfect mix of culture and sea

About the Package

Discover the magic of Spain through three unforgettable cities! From Gaudí's architecture in Barcelona to museums and royal palaces in Madrid, and the crystal-clear beaches of Costa del Sol. The perfect combination of culture, history, and sea awaits you on this unforgettable journey.

Itinerary

Day 1 - Departure to Barcelona

Flight from Belgrade to Barcelona. Afternoon arrival. Hotel check-in. Brief introduction to the city.

Day 2 - Barcelona - Gaudí and Modernism

Visit Sagrada Família, Park Güell, Casa Batlló. Walk along La Rambla. Visit the Gothic Quarter and cathedral.

Day 3 - Barcelona - Madrid

Morning visit to Camp Nou stadium. Train to Madrid. Afternoon tour of the center – Puerta del Sol, Plaza Mayor.

Day 4 - Madrid - Art and Culture

Visit the Prado Museum and Reina Sofia. Explore the Royal Palace. Walk in Retiro Park.

Day 5 - Madrid - Málaga

Morning flight to Málaga on Costa del Sol. Hotel check-in. Free afternoon on the beach.

Day 6 - Málaga - Costa del Sol

Free day on Costa del Sol beaches. Optional visit to Marbella or Nerja. Farewell dinner with flamenco show.

Day 7 - Return to Belgrade

Airport transfer and flight back to Belgrade.
