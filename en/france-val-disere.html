France - Val d'Isère

Exclusive skiing in the heart of French Alps

About the Package

Discover the charm and elegance of French Alps skiing! Val d'Isère offers world-class slopes, authentic French alpine culture, and exceptional cuisine. Experience the perfect blend of challenging skiing, luxury accommodations, and the renowned French art de vivre in one of Europe's most prestigious ski resorts.

Travel Itinerary

Day 1 - Arrival in Val d'Isère

Flight to Lyon and transfer to Val d'Isère. Hotel check-in, ski resort orientation and first evening in the French Alps.

Day 2-3 - Skiing in Val d'Isère

Two full days of skiing on the prestigious slopes of Val d'Isère. World-class ski runs and spectacular Alpine views.

Day 4 - Val d'Isère - Tignes

Morning skiing in Val d'Isère. Afternoon exploration of the connected Tignes resort - experiencing the full Espace Killy ski area.

Day 5-6 - Espace Killy exploration

Two days exploring the vast Espace Killy ski area. Over 300km of slopes, glacier skiing, and authentic French mountain cuisine.

Day 7 - Departure

Free morning for last-minute shopping or spa treatments. Transfer to Lyon airport and flight back to Belgrade.
