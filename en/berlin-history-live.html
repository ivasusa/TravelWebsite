Berlin - History Alive

The city that changed the world - from division to unification

About the Package

Discover the fascinating history of Berlin, a city at the center of major 20th-century events. Walk along the remnants of the Berlin Wall, visit Brandenburg Gate, and immerse yourself in the rich cultural scene.

Itinerary

Day 1 - Arrival & City Center

Arrival in Berlin, hotel check-in, tour of Brandenburg Gate and Unter den Linden.

Day 2 - History

Berlin Wall remnants, Checkpoint Charlie, Topography of Terror, Bundestag.

Day 3 - Culture

Museum Island, Alexanderplatz, TV Tower, Berlin neighborhoods.

Day 4 - Departure

Free time for shopping, lunch in a traditional restaurant, departure.
