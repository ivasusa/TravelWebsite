Budapest - Pearl of the Danube

Thermal cities, Art Nouveau architecture, and unforgettable nightlife

About the Package

Enjoy thermal baths, architecture, and the nightlife of Budapest, the city connecting Buda and Pest over the beautiful Danube. Discover why this city is known as the "Paris of the East".

Itinerary

Day 1 - Arrival & Pest

Arrival in Budapest, hotel check-in, tour of Pest including Parliament and St. Stephen's Basilica.

Day 2 - Buda & Thermal Baths

Buda Castle, Fisherman's Bastion, visit to Széchenyi or Gellért Thermal Baths.

Day 3 - Culture & Entertainment

Great Market Hall, State Opera, Danube cruise with dinner.

Day 4 - Departure

Morning walk along Váci Street, souvenir shopping, departure home.
